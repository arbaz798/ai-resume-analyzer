About AI Resume Analyzer

Project Overview

The AI-Powered Resume Analyzer is a web-based application designed to help job seekers improve their resumes using artificial intelligence and natural language processing (NLP) techniques. Our tool analyzes uploaded resumes and provides detailed, personalized feedback to increase your chances of landing job interviews.

How It Works

1. Upload

Upload your resume in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format

2. Analyze

Our AI analyzes your resume using advanced NLP techniques

3. Improve

Receive personalized feedback and suggestions for improvement

Key Features

  • Grammar and Spelling Check

    Identifies grammar errors and typos that could hurt your credibility

  • Structure Analysis

    Evaluates the organization and formatting of your resume

  • Language Strength Evaluation

    Identifies weak phrases and suggests stronger alternatives

  • Keyword Optimization

    Checks for industry-relevant keywords that ATS systems look for

  • Comprehensive Scoring

    Provides an overall score and component scores to track improvements

  • Actionable Suggestions

    Offers specific recommendations to enhance your resume

Technologies Used

Core Technologies:

  • Flask
Libraries:
  • NLTK
  • SpaCy
  • LanguageTool
  • PyMuPDF
